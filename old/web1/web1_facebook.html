Facebook

Facebook.com, Inc. (stylized as FACEBOOK) is an American social media conglomerate corporation based in Menlo Park, California.

Company Info

  • Industry: Social media, Advertising
  • Founded: February 4, 2004
  • Founders: Mark Zuckerberg, Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, Chris Hughes
  • Revenue: 70.697 billion US dollar (2019)
